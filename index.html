HIIVE GLOBE

HIVE International Program idea supports less privileged elementary students by providing high quality education, books, stationery, uniforms, shoes, mid-day meals, and health care. Since 2019, over 800 students have been helped through this program in Sri Lanka to have a brighter future!. The majority of our students coming out of our program has graduated from high school and is perusing a college education.
The HIIVE Board is pleased with the academic performance of our students.
